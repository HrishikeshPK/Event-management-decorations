Birthday Parties in Kerala

+91 -859 -001-0011

Creating Magical Moments: Top Birthday Event Management in Kerala by the Best Organizers

Birthday event management in Kerala for children and for adults both require adequate planning. If you plan it yourself you are tired doing the decorations, busy cooking the food or busy serving the food and this could eventually lead to a lot of confusion not forgetting the tedious job of cleaning up after the party is over. Instead of going through the tiresome process of DIYs which may or may not be successful, it is best to hire a birthday even planner who can help you achieve your goals with little or no difficulties. Melodia Events is an event management company in Kerala having special expertise in handling birthday organising which can be for either adults or children.

Planning the basics Melodia Events will help you pick a theme for the party and will procure birthday supplies according to the theme that is decided upon. Based upon the theme, we will decide on what food to prepare and games or other activities that can be organized. For children, we can also arrange for character parties such as Barbie, sponge Bob etc.

Venue and duration- As a birthday event management group, we will help you decide on the venue and the duration of the party. Your home may be too small to hold the party in which case a hotel, hall or a similar venue would be a better choice. We even have lawn and outdoor venues birthday parties for those who have barbecue yards or wish to celebrate their birthday on the poolside.

Gifts and supplies- Melodia Event management will obtain birthday party supplies such as party hats, return gifts, candles, props etc. We will also supply the necessary audio-visual set up systems with songs.

Entertainment, anchor and DJ –A birthday party is a incomplete without an entertainer. We will provide magicians, mascots, face painters and tattoo artists for a children’s birthday party. We can also provide with lighting and DJs where required. Melodia Events professional anchors will project expressive speeches and presentations that will boost audience morale and keep them enthralled. At the end of the show, children along with the rest of the audience will be encouraged to dance for the party.

Birthday Parties Decoration service–Melodia Event Management Company offers birthday decoration services in Kerala. Our team specializes in creating stunning birthday stage decorations that will make your event memorable. Contact us to add the perfect touch to your celebration.
